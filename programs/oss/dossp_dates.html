DOSSP Dates & Timeline

This page outlines key dates for the Tech Lead application process and the required pre-semester workshop.

Upcoming: Fall 2026​

  • Application Deadline: July 30, 2026
  • Selection Notification: Rolling, starting April 2026
  • BOLT Workshop: Mid-August 2026 (details TBD); a May session may also be offered if there is interest
  • Classes Begin: Wednesday, August 19, 2026

Seats are limited. We encourage interested students to apply early. Contact oss@slu.edu with any questions.

Past Cohorts​

Spring 2026​

  • Selection Notification: December 10, 2025
  • BOLT Workshop Session 1: December 15-19, 2025
  • BOLT Workshop Session 2: January 5-9, 2026

Fall 2025​

  • Selection Notification: August 1, 2025
  • BOLT Workshop: August 11-19, 2025

Spring 2025​

  • Selection Notification: December 20, 2024
  • BOLT Workshop: January 2-7, 2025

Fall 2024​

  • Selection Notification: July 26, 2024
  • BOLT Workshop: August 5-16, 2024
note

Tech Lead cohorts before Fall 2024 were not part of the DOSSP course. The Tech Lead role has been part of the program since Fall 2022.
