Participate in BOLT

BOLT is open to multiple audiences. Find the pathway that fits.

Required for Tech Leads

Individuals selected as Tech Leads for the Opening Skills and Solutions program must participate in the BOLT workshop. This training provides essential preparation for their leadership responsibilities.

See Developing Open Source Software Products (DOSSP) for full details on the Tech Lead role and how to apply.

Open Applications

Individuals interested in participating in BOLT may apply for available spots beyond those reserved for Tech Leads. Participation is not guaranteed and requires a short application and a small registration fee. This ensures participants are committed to engaging fully with the intensive workshop experience.

Custom Workshops

Research labs or other groups interested in BOLT training should contact us to discuss custom workshop arrangements tailored to specific needs and context.

Apply​

Tech Lead applicants: Complete the DOSSP application form. Accepted Tech Leads are automatically enrolled in the next BOLT session.

Open applicants: Contact oss@slu.edu to inquire about available spots for the upcoming session.

Custom workshops: Contact oss@slu.edu to discuss your group's needs.

See Dates and Timeline for the upcoming session schedule.
