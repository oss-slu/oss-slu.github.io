## Lead the Next Generation of Software Developers

Join our experiential learning program where graduate students serve as Tech Leads, mentoring undergraduate teams while developing real-world open source software products. This course combines technical leadership, project management, and software development in a dynamic, collaborative environment.

<details>
<summary>Highlights</summary>

- Approximately 12+ hours/week commitment across the semester
- Begins with the Building Open Leadership Toolsets (BOLT) pre-semester workshop ($500 stipend)
- Satisfies Software Engineering, Advanced Applications, or general CS elective requirements
- Open to CS, Software Engineering, STEM Entrepreneurship, and other graduate programs with relevant experience
- Paid development opportunities through our developer bench

</details>
