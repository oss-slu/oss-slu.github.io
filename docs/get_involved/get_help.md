Open Source with SLU provides research software engineering expertise, training, and consulting for researchers and organizations. Whether you need help with a grant proposal, workforce development strategy, software architecture, or open science practices, we can help.

## Collaborative Research Proposals

**Partner with us on your next grant.**

Looking for open source, research software engineering, or open data expertise for a proposal? We collaborate with PIs on grant development, contributing technical strategy, software architecture plans, and budget justification for RSE components.

Contact us at [oss@slu.edu](mailto:oss@slu.edu) to start a conversation about your upcoming proposal.

## Early Access to Fresh Talent

**See emerging developers in action before anyone else does.**

Organizations can work with our development teams on real projects and get a firsthand look at the next generation of software talent. You see how people work, what they build, and whether they're a fit for your organization. No interviews required.

<details>
<summary>More info...</summary>

- Sponsor a development team working on projects aligned with your interests.
- Observe skills, work habits, and collaboration style through actual project delivery.
- Open Source with SLU handles all coordination. You engage as much or as little as you want.
- We can also talk about custom training, open source adoption strategy, or other workforce goals if that's of interest.
- Contact [oss@slu.edu](mailto:oss@slu.edu) to discuss options.

</details>

## Research Software Engineering

Work directly with our research software engineers to develop, refactor, or sustain software critical to your research. We handle architecture, implementation, testing, and documentation so your team can focus on the science.

## Clinics and Workshops

**Hands-on training tailored to your needs.**

Visit our weekly drop-in clinics for one-on-one help, or request a workshop for your lab or department. Topics range from version control and testing to reproducible research workflows and software architecture.

<details>
<summary>More info...</summary>

- **Drop-in clinics**: Weekly sessions. Bring your code, your questions, or just curiosity.
- **Custom workshops**: We come to your lab or department with training tailored to your tools and goals.
- **Topics include**: Git/GitHub workflows, testing strategies, containerization, CI/CD, documentation practices, and more.
- No cost for SLU researchers. External availability varies.

</details>

[Find out about clinics and workshops ⇨](./training.md)

<details>
