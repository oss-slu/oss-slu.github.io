This page outlines key dates for the Tech Lead application process and the required pre-semester workshop.

## Upcoming: Fall 2026

- **Application Deadline:** July 30, 2026
- **Selection Notification:** Rolling, starting April 2026
- **BOLT Workshop:** Mid-August 2026 (details TBD); a May session may also be offered if there is interest
- **Classes Begin:** Wednesday, August 19, 2026

Seats are limited. We encourage interested students to apply early. Contact [oss@slu.edu](mailto:oss@slu.edu) with any questions.
