## Semester Rhythm

Each capstone semester runs 16 weeks and follows a consistent structure:

- **Weeks 1-2:** Team formation, project onboarding, environment setup
- **Weeks 3-13:** Sprint-based development cycles with regular milestones
- **Weeks 14-15:** Final presentations, documentation wrap-up, and handoff
- **Week 16:** Launch Day
