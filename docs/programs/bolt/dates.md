A May session may also be offered if there is interest. Contact [oss@slu.edu](mailto:oss@slu.edu) for the latest information.

## Session Format

Each BOLT session covers ten themes across approximately two weeks of half-day sessions (typically 1:00-5:00 PM). A break between the two halves gives participants time for independent deep-dive work on their assigned projects. The break length varies by session, from a weekend to a full week.

See [Workshop Content](/programs/bolt/workshop) for detailed themes and artifacts.
