Corporate partners, nonprofits, and institutional collaborators can engage with Open Source with SLU to build software, develop talent pipelines, and advance open source adoption.

## Build Software Together

Propose a software project and get a dedicated student development team, guided by experienced mentors and tech leads. Your project becomes a learning platform for students and a real product for your organization. [Propose a project ⇨](../get_involved/solutions.md)

## Discover Emerging Talent

Access a managed talent pipeline of students trained in real-world software development, open source practices, and team collaboration. [Discover talent ⇨](../get_involved/talent.md)

## Place Industry Fellows

Send experienced professionals from your organization to mentor our student teams. Industry Fellows gain leadership experience and community visibility while your organization builds relationships with emerging talent. [Learn about the Industry Fellows program ⇨](../get_involved/mentoring.md)

## Sponsor Events

Support hackathons, demo days, workshops, and community gatherings that bring together students, professionals, and the open source community. Event sponsorship offers direct visibility with participants and attendees. [Contact us about event sponsorship ⇨](mailto:oss@slu.edu)

## Engage Our Programs

Explore deeper, sponsored engagement with Open Source with SLU — from co-developing curricula and hosting collaborative workshops to integrating open source practices into your organization's workflow. [Learn about program engagement ⇨](../get_involved/engage.md)

## Sponsor Our Work

Invest in the future of open source education and innovation through financial sponsorship or in-kind contributions. [Become a sponsor ⇨](../get_involved/sponsorship.md)
