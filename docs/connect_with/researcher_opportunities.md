Faculty, post-docs, and research labs can access expert support for research software, open data, and open science — whether you need hands-on help, strategic guidance, or a collaborator on your next proposal.

## Develop Research Proposals Together

Looking for open source, open data, or research software engineering expertise for your next grant? We collaborate with PIs on research proposals, contributing technical strategy, software architecture, and budget justification for RSE components. [Get in touch ⇨](mailto:oss@slu.edu)

## Get Expert Help

Work with our experienced research software engineers on your current project. [Research software consulting ⇨](../get_involved/rse.md)

Attend clinics and workshops on research software engineering best practices. [Clinics and workshops ⇨](../get_involved/training.md)

## Open Science & Data

Develop a data management plan that meets funder requirements and follows FAIR principles. [Open data planning ⇨](../get_involved/data.md)

Get guidance on making your research more open, reproducible, and impactful. [Open science consulting ⇨](../get_involved/open_science.md)

## Propose a Capstone Project

Have a software need for your research? Propose it as a student capstone project and get a dedicated development team working under experienced mentors. [Propose a project ⇨](../get_involved/solutions.md)
