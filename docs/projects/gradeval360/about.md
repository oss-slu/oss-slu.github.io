## Overview

GradEval360 is a centralized performance management platform for Saint Louis University's Graduate Assistants. It facilitates the complete GA evaluation lifecycle through structured workflows that enable mentors and graduate assistants to set expectations, conduct self-evaluations, and manage performance reviews.
