Innovation Challenge: Raspberry Pi Application for Research Support

February 15, 2024 · 2 min read
Daniel Shown
Program Director for Open Source with SLU

Research labs are filled with real innovation opportunities, from data acquisition to automation infrastructure. This is your chance to help accelerate discovery.

What: $500 Innovation Challenge: Raspberry Pi Application for Research Support
Who: Saint Louis University students
When: Spring 2024
Where: Saint Louis University
Resources: Sign Up

Get ready to unleash your creativity and make a real impact to SLU research. We're calling all students to participate in a competition to create applications using Raspberry Pi technology. Your creations must be game-changers for real research labs. Some examples are: replacing manual work with a Raspberry Pi based automation, using sensors to collect data, alerting on something, achieving something new, etc.

Here's how it works:

  1. Form a Team or Compete Individually
  2. Identify an Opportunity
  3. Propose a Solution
  4. Finalists Announced on Pi Day
  5. Build your Solution
  6. Win Cash and Glory

Deadlines​

  • Proposal form submissions are due by 11:59pm on March 4.
  • Finalists will be announced on 3.14, Pi Day, March 14.
  • Project builds must be submitted by April 22.
  • Winners will bew announced at the SSE Senior Showcase, April 24.
